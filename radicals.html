部首 ・ ぶしゅ

Radicals (部首) are the recurring graphical components of kanji, and the traditional Kangxi set numbers exactly 214. Dictionaries index kanji by them, and they work like roots in a word: 茶 (tea) is grass + tree + a person sheltering. Recognising the common ones turns complex kanji from random strokes into readable structure. Search by meaning, browse by stroke count, and tap any to hear its Japanese name.
