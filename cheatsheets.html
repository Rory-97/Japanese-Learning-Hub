個

Counters

助数詞 — josūshi

Japanese can't just say "three dogs" — you pick a counter that matches the kind of thing. The big ones:

CounterForWatch out
〜つgeneral objects (1–10)ひとつ, ふたつ… とお
〜こ (個)small / round objectsいっこ, ろっこ, はっこ
〜にん (人)people1 ひとり, 2 ふたり, then 〜にん
〜ほん (本)long, cylindrical thingsいっぽん, さんぼん, ろっぽん
〜まい (枚)flat, thin things (paper)regular: いちまい…
〜ひき (匹)small animalsいっぴき, さんびき, ろっぴき
〜だい (台)machines, vehiclesregular
〜さつ (冊)books, bound volumesいっさつ, はっさつ
〜はい (杯)cupfuls / glassfulsいっぱい, さんばい, ろっぱい
〜かい (回)times / occurrencesいっかい, ろっかい
〜さい (歳)age (years old)いっさい, はっさい, 20 = はたち

💡 When in doubt for objects, the native 〜つ set (ひとつ, ふたつ…) works for most things up to ten.

こ

こそあど words

this · that · that over there · which

A beautifully regular system: こ = near me, そ = near you, あ = far from both, ど = question. Learn the pattern once, get five sets free.

Meaningこ (near me)そ (near you)あ (over there)ど (which?)
thingこれそれあれどれ
+ nounこのそのあのどの
placeここそこあそこどこ
direction (polite)こちらそちらあちらどちら
kind ofこんなそんなあんなどんな
mannerこうそうああどう
